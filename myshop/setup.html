1. Connect USB Drive
Plug the USB flash drive you used in your old computer.
The system will automatically detect it and click the input menu and select a secure StockApp*_Backups .zip folder.
2. Automatic Protection
You don't need to do anything!
As long as the drive is plugged in, the system will silently restore your inventory and sales data from the old system which was saved in the USB flash drive.

Lost data? No problem.
Simply select a backup file from the list below and click Restore. Your data will be recovered instantly.
