Wishing You a Happy Labour Day

Celebrate the holiday by adding our limited edition Labour Day banner to your customer receipts. Follow the guide below to enable the feature.

Tutorial: Enable Labour Day Banner

Quick Setup Guide:

  • 1

    Open your Settings menu from the main dashboard.

  • 2

    Select the Feature Tab (Fetvtebge) from the sidebar.

  • 3

    Find the Labour Day option and turn it ON.

  • 4

    Click Save to apply changes.

🎉 Success! Every receipt will now contain the Labour Day Banner.
